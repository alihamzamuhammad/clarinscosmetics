We guarantee 100% Authenticity of our products. Our products are sourced either from the principal companies or the authorized distributors/suppliers of the brands

Q: “Do I need to sign in to place an order?”

You can create an account or order from Guest Checkout. We would however encourage you to create an account so that you can avail all the offers we have exclusively for our online audience.

You can message us on Facebook, Instagram, or Whatsapp or call us at 0300-1234567 to confirm your order. Alternatively, you could wait for us to call you, we call thrice before cancelling a customer-placed order.
