Privacy Policy

At Clarins Cosmetic, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to safeguard your data.

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and billing information when you sign up for our services, make a purchase, or contact us. We use this information to process your orders, provide customer support, and send promotional materials or newsletters, if you have opted in to receive them.

Our website may also use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, collect anonymous usage statistics, and enable certain features. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. By using our website, you agree to our use of cookies as described in our Cookie Policy.

We implement a variety of security measures to protect your personal information. Access to your personal information is limited to employees and third-party service providers who need it to perform their duties. We also use secure technologies, such as SSL encryption, to protect sensitive information during transmission.

We may share your personal information with third parties for the purposes of order fulfillment, payment processing, and providing other services on our behalf. We require these third parties to protect your personal information and use it only for the purposes for which it was provided.

Our website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any website you visit.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about our privacy practices.
